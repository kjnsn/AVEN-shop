About this website

Asexuality is a sexual orientation (eg: heterosexuality, homosexuality, bisexuality or pansexuality) in which one does not experience attraction of a sexual nature to anybody, regardless of their gender. It is also known as the ‘invisible orientation’, due to its relative rarity (it is estimated that 1% of the worlds’ population is asexual) and lack of visibility.

This is changing however, with the appearance of asexual contingents at pride parades, queer conferences and LGBTQ+ communities. As visibility increases, so does the demand for asexual pride and visibility materials such as badges, T-shirts and flags. This webpage aims to provide a place where people can easily find asexuality-related gifts, clothes and jewellery, making it a valuable resource for both asexual visibility activists, who will need to purchase goods for pride parades and events, and the general public, who might be searching for an asexuality-related gift.

The website also strives to provide links to the well-known asexuality organisation, AVEN (otherwise known as the Asexual Visibility and Education Network), which in turn hosts the largest asexual community in discussion forums, chat and wiki. This store, therefore, will become the latest addition to AVEN’s online presence, aiding it in its mission to increase asexual visibility, acceptance and education in the world.
To do this, the website must be:

  • User friendly (intuitive links and design, with an opportunity for feedback)
  • Up-to-date
  • Professional-looking in design, to reflect AVEN’s status as an organisation
  • Relevant (only goods that are related to asexuality)

In this way, this webpage may be a useful way to promote asexual visibility, and a great way to facilitate distribution of asexuality-related merchandise on and offline.
